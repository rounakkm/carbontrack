About CarbonTrack

CarbonTrack is an AI-driven platform designed to help individuals monitor, analyze, and reduce their carbon footprint. Our goal is to empower users with real-time insights and smart recommendations for a greener lifestyle.

Our Services

Service 1

Uses AI-powered text extraction to analyze receipts and bills, estimating carbon footprints automatically.

Service 2

Leverages AI algorithms to break down your daily activities and identify high-carbon habits.

Service 3

Suggests personalized carbon offset solutions, like tree planting or green investments.
